LAN

In addition to Tor, you can connect with your Embassy over the Local Area Network (LAN), which offers a faster experience.

  1. Download and trust your Embassy's Root Certificate Authority (below).
  2. Visit your Embassy's LAN Address from the browser (below).

You must be connected to the same LAN as your Embassy for this to work. View the complete instructions here.
